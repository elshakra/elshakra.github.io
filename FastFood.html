Exam season is one of the most hectic times in the semester. During exams, I get so caught up in studying I either forget to eat, or don't have enough time to cook. Exam season is the time where fast food restaurants come in handy!

My Favorite Fast Food Place

Raising Cane's Chicken is my favorite fast food place on campus. The food is great, and the seating area is really nice. It's conviently located extremly close to two of the main libraries on campus
