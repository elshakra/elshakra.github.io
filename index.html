Hi everyone! I am a sophomore at the University of Wisconsin Madison. One of my biggest struggles when I first moved to Madison was what to eat and where to eat. I am a huge foodie, and love exploring new restaurants. Due to this, I decided to create this website to help people first moving to Madison as well as visitors of the city choose where to eat based on the options available around campus.
